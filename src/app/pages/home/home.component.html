FAQ

Common Questions from GTA Businesses

How does the 30-Day Guarantee work?
For the Growth or Business plan, if the first 30 days do not deliver a clear stability improvement, you can cancel with no penalty. Transparent monthly pricing, no hidden fees, no surprise invoices.
Can you take over from our current IT provider?
Yes. We run a structured transition covering credentials, tenant access, asset inventory, backup validation, and support handoff. Clean takeover with minimal disruption to your staff.
How fast do you respond when something breaks?
Urgent production-impact incidents are triaged immediately. Growth and Business clients receive response within 15 minutes, with remote-first response and on-site escalation when required.
How much does managed IT support cost in Vaughan?
Three flat-rate plans: Starter at $249/month, Growth at $499/month with priority response, and Business at $899/month with full IT department outsourcing. All transparent — no hidden fees.
What cities do you provide IT support in?
We serve Vaughan, Toronto, Mississauga, Thornhill, and Richmond Hill with both remote support and on-site visits throughout York Region, Peel Region, and the downtown Toronto core.
Do you support both Google Workspace and Microsoft 365?
Yes. Certified administrators for both platforms — user accounts, email security, shared drive permissions, data retention policies, and device access controls.
What cybersecurity tools do you use?
We deploy Huntress for AI-assisted endpoint detection, Tailscale for zero-trust network access, and enforce MFA, conditional access policies, and regular vulnerability scanning across all managed endpoints.
Do you support law firms, medical clinics, and accounting firms?
Yes. Dedicated expertise in regulated industries — Law Society compliance for law firms, PHIPA-compliant infrastructure for medical clinics, CRA-compliant data handling for accounting firms.
