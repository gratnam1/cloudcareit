# Best IT Support for Law Firms in Toronto: What to Look For

Law firms in Toronto face unique IT challenges. You aren't just protecting files; you're protecting client confidentiality, maintaining Law Society of Ontario compliance, and ensuring your billing and document management systems never go down.

When evaluating an IT support provider for your law firm, here are the critical factors you need to look for.

## 1. Law Society Compliance Experience
Your IT provider must understand the Law Society of Ontario's guidelines on technology and security. They need to know how to structure your data, secure your communications, and manage user access to keep your firm compliant.

## 2. Cybersecurity-First Approach
Law firms are prime targets for ransomware and phishing because they hold highly sensitive data and large amounts of money in trust accounts. Your IT provider must offer:
- **Advanced EDR (Endpoint Detection and Response)**
- **Strict MFA Enforcement**
- **Zero-Trust Network Access**
- **Encrypted Communications**

## 3. Expertise in Legal Practice Management Software
Your provider shouldn't be learning how your software works on your dime. They need experience supporting core legal applications:
- PC Law
- Clio
- Relativity
- NetDocuments
- Worldox

## 4. Bulletproof Backup & Disaster Recovery
A ransomware attack can halt court filings and freeze billable hours. Your provider must implement immutable, off-site backups with a tested, documented Disaster Recovery plan ensuring your firm can recover in hours, not weeks.

## 5. Rapid Response SLA
When an associate is trying to file a document at 11:59 PM and the VPN goes down, you need an IT partner who answers the phone immediately. Look for guarantees on response times.
