# Managed IT vs Break-Fix IT: Which Is Right for Your GTA Business?

If you run a business in the Greater Toronto Area with 5 to 50 employees, you eventually hit a wall with your IT. When you do, you face a choice between two models of IT support: Break-Fix or Managed IT Services (MSP).

## What is Break-Fix IT?
Exactly what it sounds like. Something breaks (a computer won't boot, the Wi-Fi is down, a server crashes), you call the IT guy, they fix it, and they send you an invoice for their hourly rate.

**The Problem with Break-Fix:**
- **Incentives are misaligned:** The IT guy only makes money when you are experiencing downtime.
- **No proactive security:** Nobody is watching the network, applying security patches, or checking the backups until disaster strikes.
- **Unpredictable costs:** Some months you pay $0. Other months you pay $5,000 for a massive emergency.

## What is Managed IT Services (MSP)?
Managed IT is a proactive model. You pay a flat, predictable monthly fee per user. In exchange, the MSP takes full responsibility for the health, security, and performance of your entire IT environment.

**The Benefits of Managed IT:**
- **Incentives are aligned:** The MSP loses money (in labor costs) if you have constant issues. It is in their best interest to ensure your network is robust, secure, and never breaks down.
- **Security-First Approach:** 24/7 monitoring, EDR installation, MFA enforcement, and secure backups are all built-in.
- **Predictable Budget:** You know exactly what your IT will cost every single month. No surprise invoices.
- **Strategic Guidance:** A good MSP provides a virtual CIO (vCIO) to help you plan technology upgrades that drive business growth.

## The Verdict
If you are a one-person shop, break-fix might be fine. But the moment your GTA business relies on its data, employs a team of professionals, and cannot afford hours of downtime or a ransomware breach, **Managed IT Services is the only logical choice.**
