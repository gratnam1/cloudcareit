LIVE CASE STUDY

AI-Powered Chauffeur Booking Platform

A Toronto-based private chauffeur service needed customers to book, modify, and cancel rides without staff involvement — over both web and phone.

Voice IVR Agent
  • Answers calls, understands natural speech — no menus
  • Creates, looks up, updates, and cancels bookings via AI tool calls
  • Detects caller frustration and auto-escalates to a human
  • Handles silence gracefully with interim TTS during processing
Intelligent Chat Booking
  • Customers describe trips in plain language
  • AI extracts pickup, destination, date, time, and package
  • Live Google Maps call for accurate distance-based pricing
  • Returns structured booking summary with fare estimate
