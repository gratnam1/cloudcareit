Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about switching your IT to CtrlShift IT Services.

How fast is your response time for emergencies?
For production-down emergencies, we guarantee a response from a qualified engineer within 15 minutes. For standard support tickets, our average response time is under 1 hour.
Do I have to sign a long-term contract?
No. We believe in earning your business every month. Our Growth and Business plans are month-to-month. If you're not happy, you can cancel at any time with 30 days' notice.
What is included in the "Flat-Rate" pricing?
Our flat-rate monthly fee covers all remote helpdesk support, 24/7 monitoring, security software (EDR), patch management, and vCIO strategy. Hardware and major one-time projects are scoped separately.
Can you help us with cyber insurance compliance?
Yes. We specialize in aligning professional offices with the stringent requirements of modern cyber insurance providers, including MFA enforcement, encrypted backups, and endpoint detection (EDR).
Do you support both Mac and Windows?
Absolutely. We use cross-platform management tools to ensure your entire fleet — whether it's a Windows workstation or a MacBook Pro — is secure, patched, and supported.
