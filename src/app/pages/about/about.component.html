Why We Started CtrlShift IT Services

After years of working in enterprise IT — building infrastructure for large organizations with proper security controls, fast response teams, and proactive monitoring — we kept seeing the same pattern: small and medium businesses in the GTA were left behind.

They were stuck with break-fix IT shops that only showed up when something was already on fire. They were paying for contracts with no SLAs. They were getting ransomware attacks and having no idea what to do. And they were being charged for hardware and hours they never agreed to.

We built CtrlShift IT Services to close that gap — to give 5-to-50-person offices in the GTA access to the same quality of IT support that enterprise companies take for granted, at a price that makes sense.

Our Mission

To be the most trusted managed IT partner for professional offices in the Greater Toronto Area — delivering proactive, cybersecurity-first IT support that eliminates downtime, protects critical data, and lets business owners focus on what they actually do.
